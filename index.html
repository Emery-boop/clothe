The Zenthrova Legacy

Founded in Milan in 1987, Zenthrova represents the pinnacle of Italian craftsmanship fused with contemporary design sensibilities. Each garment is a testament to our heritage of excellence.

Our atelier employs master tailors with decades of experience, utilizing techniques passed down through generations while innovating for the modern wardrobe.

We source only the finest materials from around the world - silks from Como, cashmere from Mongolia, and wools from the Scottish Highlands - ensuring unparalleled quality and luxury.
